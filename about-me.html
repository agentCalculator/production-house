Raysun Productions

From Small Beginnings to Silver Screens, Founded by Director Radhika Jayanthi, Raysun Productions began with a simple vision: to tell powerful stories with honesty, emotion, and artistic courage. Starting with small-scale music videos and independent shorts, we stepped into international acclaim with MAAYA, a debut directorial venture filmed in the USA that won multiple awards.

Today, RaysuN Productions proudly presents its first Telugu feature film — a milestone built on passion, perseverance, and purpose. We’re not just making movies; we’re building a legacy of heartfelt cinema.

Film Production

From concept to final cut, we handle every stage of film production with a focus on cinematic excellence. Whether it's a commercial, music video, short film, or documentary—our team brings your vision to life with precision and passion.

New Technologies

We embrace cutting-edge tools like 4K cameras, drone cinematography, and virtual production techniques to ensure your story is told in the most immersive and modern way possible.

Top Directions

Led by visionary directors and creative leads, our projects reflect strong narratives, powerful visuals, and purposeful storytelling that resonate with diverse audiences.

Distribution

Our work doesn’t end at production. We help you navigate the landscape of digital and traditional distribution to get your content in front of the right viewers at the right time.
