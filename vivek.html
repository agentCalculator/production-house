Vivek Vardhan Kanduri

Creative Advisor & Script Consultant

The Silent Strength Behind the Scenes Vivek Vardhan Kanduri is more than just a creative contributor to Raysun Productions — he is its emotional anchor and an ever-reliable guiding voice. As the Creative Advisor and Script Consultant, Vivek’s input has shaped the heart and flow of Rayavalasa, infusing it with thought, nuance, and balance. The younger brother of Director Radhika Jayanthi, Vivek has been a constant pillar of encouragement and ideation since the production house’s earliest steps. Despite working full-time in Dubai due to family responsibilities, his unwavering love for cinema remains at his core. With an intuitive sense of story and character, he is always ready to offer powerful insights, valuable feedback, and immediate solutions — no matter the distance. Vivek’s deep passion for performance finally came alive in Rayavalasa, where he made his acting debut. His natural presence on screen mirrors the hidden fire he has always carried for storytelling and performance. Though his career has followed a different path, Vivek remains an inseparable part of Raysun Productions’ creative spirit, offering support that goes beyond titles — as a brother, mentor, and silent strength behind every scene.

Cinema has always been my silent passion — even from afar, I’m part of every story and every scene.

- Vivek Vardhan Kanduri
